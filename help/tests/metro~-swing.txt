To turn 3 pulses into 4, modulate tempo with an amplitude of 1/3,
which will result in 1 pulse that is twice as long as the other two pulses.
Example: 3 pulses that are 333.333 ms each:
```
	long pulses  = (1 * 1000/3) / (1 - 1/3) = 500 ms
	short pulses = (2 * 1000/3) / (1 + 1/3) = 500 ms
```
To turn 4 pulses into 6, reframe the problem as turning 8 pulses into 12,
then modulate tempo with an amplitude of 1/2, resulting in a long pulse that is
3 times longer than a short pulse, of which there are 2 and 6 respectively.
Example: 8 pulses that are 125 ms each:
```
	long pulses  = (2 * 125) / (1 - 1/2) = 500 ms
	short pulses = (6 * 125) / (1 + 1/2) = 500 ms
```
Then reinterpret the pattern. Example:
```
	8-to-12: ||..|..||||||
	4-to-6:  |...|...|.|.|
	2-to-3:  |.......|...|
```
